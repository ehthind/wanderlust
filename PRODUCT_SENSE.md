# Product Sense

## Primary persona
- couples planning meaningful trips
- one person usually plans on behalf of the pair

## Wanderlust defaults
- social is light in v1
- reliable bookability matters more than maximal inventory complexity
- guest-first identity is the default
- Expedia-first booking is the default lodging spine in v1

## Core product objects
- DestinationSummary
- DestinationProfile
- EditorialStory
- FutureTripList
- TripDraft
- InboxThreadSummary
